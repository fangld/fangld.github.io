# Bio

Liangda Fang received his BSc, MSc and PhD from [Guangzhou University](https://english.gzhu.edu.cn/), [Guangdong University of Technology](https://english.gdut.edu.cn/) and [Sun Yat-sen University](https://www.sysu.edu.cn/sysuen/) in 2007, 2010 and 2015, respectively. After graduaction, he joined [Jinan University](https://english.jnu.edu.cn/) as a lecturer, and was promoted to an associate professor in 2018. His reasearch is in the area of artificial intelligence including knowledge representation and reasoning, machine learning, planning, multi-agent systems and AI for education. Liangda Fang has published 43 papers (including 24 CCF Rank A papers and 19 CCF Rank B papers). He served as one of the local chairs of [SoCS-2021](https://sites.google.com/unibs.it/socs2021), as (Senior) PC member or Reviewer of [AAAI](https://aaai.org/Conferences/AAAI/aaai.php) (2019-2025), [CVPR](https://cvpr.thecvf.com/) (2025), [ECAI](https://www.eurai.org/ecai) (2020, 2023-2025), [ICML](https://icml.cc/) (2022), [IJCAI](https://www.ijcai.org/) (2018, 2020, 2021, 2023-2025), [KR](https://www.kr.org/) (2021), [NeurIPS](https://neurips.cc/) (2022), [WSDM](https://www.wsdm-conference.org/) (2023) and [WWW](https://www2024.thewebconf.org/) (2024). He also received the Distinguished (Senior) PC Award of [IJCAI-2021](https://ijcai-21.org/distinguished-reviewers/) and [IJCAI-2023](https://ijcai-23.org/distinguished-program-committee-members/), the Outstanding Reviewer Award of ICML-2022, and the Discussion Master Award of ECAI-2023.
